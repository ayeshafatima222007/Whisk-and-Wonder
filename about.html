Our Story

How We Started

Whisk and Wonder began with nothing but a single wire whisk, a dusting of flour, and a big dream in a sun-drenched kitchen in Lahore. What started as an experiment in perfecting the sourdough loaf turned into a passion for sharing authentic flavors. We wanted to create bread that doesn't just fill the stomach, but tastes like the warm, comforting childhood memories of Sunday mornings.
